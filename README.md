## ðŸ“‰ Executive Summary: The "Split-Brain" Risk
**Problem:** The current market strategy applies a "one-size-fits-all" Live Service model to disparate asset classes. This approach has diluted the brand identity of legacy pillars like *Battlefield*.

**The Financial Impact:**
Misalignment of the *Battlefield* IP (forcing a "Tactical Shooter" into a "Battle Royale" Red Ocean) creates significant enterprise risk:
* **Market Cap Volatility:** The *Battlefield 2042* launch window demonstrated how legacy IP instability can trigger **$5B+ in Market Cap Erosion** despite strong performance in other sectors.
* **LTV Decay:** Alienation of the "Core Veteran" demographic reduces long-tail recurring revenue (Customer Lifetime Value).

**The Solution:**
A **Dual-Track Asset Strategy** that decouples "High-Velocity Servicers" (FC, Madden) from "Prestige Pillars" (Battlefield), supported by next-gen **DMA-Resistant Integrity Architecture**.
